Hokkien

Characteristics

Characteristics

Hokkien Cuisine has four main types, namely the Fuzhou Style, Western Fujian Style, Southern Fujian Style and Quanzhou Style. The Fuzhou style has the lightest taste and is most famous for its variety of soups. Western and Southern Fujian are two similar styles. They both contain spicy and sweet tastes, with an elaborate use of sauces and condiments. Main cooking methods for these two styles include steaming, frying and stir-frying. Lastly, there is the Quanzhou style. It is widely seen as the healthiest style, with dishes here being the least oily but with the strongest flavour.
